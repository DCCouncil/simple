§ 39-502. Rulemaking.

The Mayor may promulgate rules necessary to implement this chapter.

History

(Mar. 14, 2007, D.C. Law 16-290, § 3, 54 DCR 984.)

Emergency Legislation

For temporary (90 day) addition, see § 3 of Film DC Economic Incentive Emergency Act of 2006 (D.C. Act 16-570, December 19, 2006, 54 DCR 8).

Legislative History of Law 16-290

For Law 16-290, see notes following § 39-501.

Delegation of Authority

Delegation of Authority under the Financial Incentives for Motion Picture and Television Productions Emergency Amendment Act of 2009, effective October 15, 2009 (D.C. Act 18-207; 56 DCR 8277), see Mayor's Order 2009-213, December 8, 2009 (56 DCR 9350).
