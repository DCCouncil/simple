§ 1-143. Staff to be used.

Wherever practicable, the District of Columbia flag shall be flown on the same staff as the United States flag.

History

(July 1, 1982, D.C. Law 4-121, § 4, 29 DCR 2072.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 1-123.
