§ 34-1130. Commission may prescribe changes in form of schedule.

The Commission may prescribe such changes in the form in which the schedules are issued by any public utility as may be found to be expedient.

History

(Mar. 4, 1913, 37 Stat. 982, ch. 150, § 8, par. 31.)
