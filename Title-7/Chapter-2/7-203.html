§ 7-203. Appointment and duties of Registrar.

The Director of the Department of Health shall appoint the Registrar who shall:

(1) Be in charge of administering the vital records system and be the custodian of its records; and

(2) Develop and distribute forms or other means for transmitting data to carry out the reporting and registration purposes of this chapter.
