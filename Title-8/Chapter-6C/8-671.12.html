§ 8-671.12. Rules.

The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, may issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.

History

(May 12, 2006, D.C. Law 16-95, § 112, 53 DCR 1652.)

Legislative History of Law 16-95

For Law 16-95, see notes following § 8-671.01.
