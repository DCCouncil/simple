§ 22-3009. Second degree child sexual abuse.

Whoever, being at least 4 years older than a child, engages in sexual contact with that child or causes that child to engage in sexual contact shall be imprisoned for not more than 10 years and, in addition, may be fined in an amount not more than the amount set forth in § 22-3571.01.

History

(May 23, 1995, D.C. Law 10-257, § 208, 42 DCR 53; June 11, 2013, D.C. Law 19-317, § 232(g), 60 DCR 2064.)

Cross References

Sentencing, supervised release, and good time credit for felonies committed on or after August 5, 2000, see § 24-403.01.

Section References

Prior Codifications

Effect of Amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-317 substituted "not more than the amount set forth in § 22-3571.01" for "in an amount not to exceed $100,000".
