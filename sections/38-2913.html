§ 38-2913. Services.

Beginning in fiscal year 2014, services provided by District of Columbia government agencies to public schools shall be provided on an equal basis to the District of Columbia Public Schools and public charter schools. Any services that are funded apart from the Uniform per Student Funding Formula shall not also be funded by the Uniform Per Student Funding Formula.

History

(Mar. 26, 1999, D.C. Law 12-207, § 115, as added Sept. 24, 2010, D.C. Law 18-223, § 4062, 57 DCR 6242; Apr. 8, 2011, D.C. Law 18-370, § 402(d), 58 DCR 1008; Sept. 20, 2012, D.C. Law 19-168, § 4072, 59 DCR 8025.)

Effect of Amendments

D.C. Law 18-370 substituted "2013" for "2012".

The 2012 amendment by D.C. Law 19-168 substituted "fiscal year 2014" for "fiscal year 2013" in the first sentence.
