§ 3–2401.05. Payment of rates for water and water service.

All rates for water and water service hereby established shall be payable at least once semiannually. When the computation of the amount of any bill for any of such services results in a fraction of one-half cent or more, the next highest amount not containing a fraction shall be charged.

History

(Oct. 21, 1975, D.C. Law 1-23, title VII, § 701(c), 22 DCR 2115.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 43-1505.

1973 Ed., § 43-1504a.

Legislative History of Law 1-23

Law 1-23 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 1-47, which was referred to the Committee on Finance and Revenue. The Bill was adopted on first, amended first, and second readings, and reconsideration of second reading, on April 15, 1975, June 1, 1975, June 24, 1975 and July 11, 1975, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 23, 1975, it was assigned Act No. 1-34 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
