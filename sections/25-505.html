§ 25-505. Fees for Arena C/X by Mayor.

The annual license fee for the retailer's licenses, class Arena C/X, for the DC Arena shall be established by the Mayor.

History

(May 3, 2001, D.C. Law 13-298, § 101, 48 DCR 2959; Mar. 3, 2010, D.C. Law 18-111, § 2082(n)(3), 57 DCR 181.)

Effect of Amendments

D.C. Law 18-111, in the section heading, deleted "and Washington Convention Center" following "C/X"; and deleted "and for the Washington Convention Center" following "DC Arena".
