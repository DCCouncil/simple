§ 50–329.01. Public vehicle inspection officer training.

Public vehicle inspection officers shall undergo training on the rules and regulations governing passenger vehicles-for-hire and undergo yearly performance evaluations. Public vehicle inspection officers shall be prohibited from making traffic stops of on-duty taxicabs while a taxicab is in the act of transporting a fare, unless there is probable cause of a violation, and shall act in accordance with all rules governing their duties, as established through rulemaking.
