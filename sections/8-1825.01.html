§ 8-1825.01. Short title.

This subchapter may be cited as the "Sustainable Urban Agriculture Apiculture Act of 2012".

History

(Apr. 20, 2013, D.C. Law 19-262, § 211, 60 DCR 1300.)

Legislative History of Law 19-262

Law 19-262, the ""Sustainable DC Amendment Act of 2012," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 19-756. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Dec. 4, 2012, and Dec. 18, 2012, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Jan. 16, 2013, it was assigned Act No. 19-615 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 19-262 became effective on Apr. 20, 2013.
