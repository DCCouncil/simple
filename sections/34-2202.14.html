§ 34-2202.14. Procurement system inapplicable.

Except as provided in § 34-2202.17(b), Chapter 3A of Title 2 [§ 2-351.01 et seq.] shall not apply to the Authority.

History

(Apr. 18, 1996, D.C. Law 11-111, § 214, 43 DCR 548; Sept. 26, 2012, D.C. Law 19-171, § 216(a), 59 DCR 6190.)

Section References

This section is referenced in § 34-2202.02.

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 43-1684.

Effect of Amendments

The 2012 amendment by D.C. Law 19-171 substituted "Chapter 3A of Title 2" for "§ 2-301.01 et seq."

Legislative History of Law 11-111

For legislative history of D.C. Law 11-111, see Historical and Statutory Notes following § 34-2201.01.

Legislative History of Law 19-171

Law 19-171, the "Technical Amendments Act of 2012," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 19-397. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Mar. 20, 2012, and Apr. 17, 2012, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on May 23, 2012, it was assigned Act No. 19-376 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 19-171 became effective on September 26, 2012.
