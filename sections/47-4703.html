§ 47-4703. Chief Financial Officer guidance.

For the preparation of the financial analysis required by § 47-4701(b)(4) and the annual certification required by § 47-4702, the Chief Financial Officer shall set forth guidance regarding the collection of information necessary to implement these sections.
