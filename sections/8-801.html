§ 8–801. Purpose of chapter.

The purpose of this chapter is to provide civil sanctions and to eliminate criminal liability for violating a variety of local laws and rules, to provide for civil enforcement of these violations, and to establish an expeditious administrative adjudicative system.

History

(Mar. 25, 1986, D.C. Law 6-100, § 2, 33 DCR 781.)

Section References

This section is referenced in § 1‑303.23, § 47‑2829, and § 47‑2862.

Legislative History of Law 6-100

Law 6-100, the "Litter Control Administrative Act of 1985," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 6-297, which was referred to the Committee on Public Works. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 17, 1985, and January 14, 1986, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on January 28, 1986, it was assigned Act No. 6-128 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
