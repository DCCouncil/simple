Legislative History of Law 1-72

Law 1-72 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 1-233, which was referred to the Advisory Committee. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 9, 1976 and March 23, 1976, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 26, 1976, it was assigned Act No. 1-108 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
