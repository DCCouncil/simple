§ 34-708. Each day's default to constitute separate and distinct offense.

Every day during which any public utility, or any officer, agent, or employee thereof, shall fail knowingly or willfully to observe and comply with any order or direction of the Commission, or to perform any duty enjoined by this section, shall constitute a separate and distinct violation of such order, or direction, or of this subtitle, as the case may be.

History

(Mar. 4, 1913, 37 Stat. 992, ch. 150, § 8, par. 87.)
