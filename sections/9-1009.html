§ 9–1009. Authority to negotiate extension of lease.

The Secretary and the Airports Authority may at any time negotiate an extension of the lease entered into under § 9‑1004(a).

History

(Oct. 30, 1986, 100 Stat. 3341-376, Pub. L. 99-591, § 6010.)

Prior Codifications
