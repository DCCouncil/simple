§ 15–914. Uniformity of application and construction.

This chapter shall be applied and construed to effectuate its general purpose to make uniform the law with respect to the subject of this chapter among states enacting similar legislation.

History

(Feb. 10, 1996, D.C. Law 11-85, § 2, 42 DCR 6791; Mar. 13, 2004, D.C. Law 15-105, § 101, 51 DCR 881; Apr. 13, 2005, D.C. Law 15-354, § 85(e), 52 DCR 2638.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 15-914.

Effect of Amendments

D.C. Law 15-105 substituted "this chapter" for "this act" in two places.

D.C. Law 15-354 substituted "this chapter" for "this subchapter".
