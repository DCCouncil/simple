§ 47-2763. Enforcement.

Any feepayer who fails to file a return or pay the ballpark fee due as required by § 47-2755 [§ 47‑2762] shall be subject to the same enforcement provisions and administrative provisions applicable to the ballpark fee as provided in Chapter 18, Chapter 41, Chapter 42 (except §§ 47‑4211(b)(1)(B), 47-4214, and 47-4215), and Chapter 43 of this title.

History

(Apr. 8, 2005, D.C. Law 15-320, § 110(f), 52 DCR 1757; Mar. 2, 2007, D.C. Law 16-191, § 80, 53 DCR 6794.)

Effect of Amendments
