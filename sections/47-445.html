§ 47-445. Rules and regulations.

The Mayor is authorized to promulgate rules and regulations necessary for the efficient administration of this subchapter.

History

(July 18, 1981, D.C. Law 4-17, § 6, 28 DCR 2368; enacted, Apr. 9, 1997, D.C. Law 11-254, § 2, 44 DCR 1575.)

Prior Codifications
