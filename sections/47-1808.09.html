§ 47-1808.09. Job growth tax credit.

A job growth tax credit shall be allowed as provided in subchapter VII-A of this chapter [§ 47-1807.56 et seq.].

History

(July 27, 2010, D.C. Law 18-202, § 2(d), 57 DCR 4746.)
