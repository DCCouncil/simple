§ 49-409. President to be Commander-in-Chief.

The President of the United States shall be the Commander-in-Chief of the militia of the District of Columbia.

History

(Mar. 1, 1889, 25 Stat. 773, ch. 328, § 6.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 39-109.

1973 Ed., § 39-112.

Editor's Notes
