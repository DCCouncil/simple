Legislative History of Law 20- (Act 20-306)

Law 20- (Act 20-306), the "DC Promise Establishment Act of 2014," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-528. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on February 4, 2014, and March 4, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on March 31, 2014 it was assigned Act No. 20-306 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20- (Act 20-306) became effective on ________, 2014.
