Legislative History of Law 2-20

Law 2-20, the "Revenue Recovery Act of 1977," was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 2-21, which was referred to the Committee on Finance and Revenue. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 3, 1977 and June 14, 1977, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 5, 1977, it was assigned Act No. 2-51 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
