Repealed.

History

(Mar. 26, 1999, D.C. Law 12-175, § 18061, as added Apr. 8, 2011, D.C. Law 18-358, § 2, 58 DCR 765; Sept. 14, 2011, D.C. Law 19-21, § 1032(a), 58 DCR 6226.)

Emergency Legislation

For temporary (90 day) repeal of section, see § 1012(a) of Fiscal Year 2012 Budget Support Emergency Act of 2011 (D.C. Act 19-93, June 29, 2011, 58 DCR 5599).

Legislative History of Law 18-358

Law 18-358, the "District Property Security Assessment and Implementation Amendment Act of 2010", was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-1058, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations and the Environment. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 7, 2010, and December 21, 2010, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on January 19, 2011, it was assigned Act No. 18-708 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-358 became effective on April 8, 2011.

For history of Law 19-21, see notes under § 10-1001.
