§ 3-209. Prosecutions.

All prosecutions under this chapter shall be in the Superior Court of the District of Columbia, on information brought in the name of said District on its behalf.

History

(Apr. 29, 1902, 32 Stat. 175, ch. 638, § 9; Apr. 1, 1942, 56 Stat. 190, ch. 207, § 1; July 8, 1963, 77 Stat. 78, Pub. L. 88-60, § 6; July 29, 1970, 84 Stat. 570, Pub. L. 91-358, title I, § 155(a).)
